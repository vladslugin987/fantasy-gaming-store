About us

Welcome to our Square Enix fan-website, a destination for Square Enix fans, gamers, and all-around nerds! Here, we're not just about offering exclusive merchandise from Final Fantasy, Kingdom Hearts, Dragon Quest, and beyond — we believe in celebrating the geek culture that unites us all. As you explore our selection of exclusive merch, take a break and enjoy a classic snake game that we’ve programmed just for you! Because sometimes, you need to take a moment to embrace the nostalgia and geek out over the classics. Plus, who doesn’t love a good challenge? The snake game is our small reminder that even in the digital world, there's always room for fun and games. Once you scroll down and use one of your keybords arrow key, the game will start.
Happy gaming, happy shopping, and may your snake never run into the walls!
