About Us

Welcome to our Square Enix fan website, a destination for Square Enix fans, gamers, and all-around nerds! Here, we're not just about offering exclusive merchandise from Final Fantasy, Kingdom Hearts, Dragon Quest, and beyond — we believe in celebrating the geek culture that unites us all.

As you explore our selection of exclusive merch, take a break and enjoy a classic snake game that we developed just for you! Sometimes you simply need a moment to embrace nostalgia and geek out over the classics. Plus, who doesn’t love a good challenge? The snake game is our reminder that even in the digital world, there's always room for fun and games.

Once you scroll down and use one of your keyboard’s arrow keys, the game will start.

Happy gaming, happy shopping, and may your snake never run into the walls!
